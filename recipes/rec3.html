Ingrediants

  • water
  • rice vinegar
  • lemon juice
  • soy sauce
  • orange juice
  • brown sugar
  • green onion
  • oragne zest
  • ginger root
  • minced garlic
  • pepper flakes
  • cornstarch
  • Most importantly! 2 large skinless chicken breast(boneless)

Steps

  1. combine water, vinegar, lemon juice, soy sauce, and oragne fuice for the sauce
  2. Stir all this in over medium heat while adding the rest of the ingrediants(not the chicken yet)
  3. After cooking let it cool for 10 to 15mins.
  4. Use a ziplock bag and add the chicken along with the cooled sauce together and let it marinate
  5. Use another bag and add the salt, pepper, and flour and mix together the chicken.
  6. Now your chicken is ready to be fried and served. (fry until browned on both sides)
