Cookies

A recipe for those who love cookies with a little extra chocolate!

Ingrediants

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips(your choice i.e. semi sweet)

Steps

  1. Mix the butter and sugars, then you'll add eggs and vanilla and repeat.
  2. Baking soda should be disolved in 'hot' water then added to the mixture.
  3. Now the flour and chocolate chips can be mixed in.
  4. Make little dough balls with this mixture, about half a golf ball.
  5. Use a baking sheet and place 1 inch apart, and bake until golden around the edges.
