Birria Tacos

For those who want to take their taco experience to the next level. Fried tacos with a dipping sauce.

Ingrediants(tacos)

  • Beef fat
  • Corn tortillas
  • Monterey jack cheese
  • Shredded beef
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Lime, cut into four

Ingrediants(consome)

  • carrots
  • chicken broth
  • beef birria sauce
  • celery and cabbage

Steps(consome)

  1. Mix all the ingrediants together in a sauce pan
  2. Once vegtables are tender bring to a simmer
  3. Let sit on simmer until tacos are complete(check occassionally)

Steps(tacos)

  1. Grab a skillet and heat up the beef fat
  2. Add two tortillas and toast each side until crispy
  3. Now you will cover with cheese and the shredded beef
  4. Now use the consome and add about a table spoon to each taco made
  5. Fold tacos together and cook for another 2 minutes
  6. Use a bowl and add consome for dipping
